ReverySky Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ReverySky (the "App"), published by MoonSkorch Studio, accesses and handles information.

1. Overview

ReverySky is designed to work primarily with data stored locally on your device.

The App does not require account registration.

Unless stated otherwise, the App does not send your files to the developer and does not share them with third parties.

2. Information the App May Access

Depending on how you use the App, it may access or process:

  • files and folders that you explicitly choose to make available to the App;
  • note files created, opened, edited, or saved through the App;
  • technical information necessary for the App to function, such as app version, operating system version, device type, and basic diagnostic or configuration data stored locally on the device.

3. Folder Access

By default, the App may save files in the App's default folder.

If you choose to use your own folder instead, the App may request access only to the folder you select. This access is used only to read existing note files and save new or updated note files in that location.

The App does not request access to folders you have not selected.

4. How Data Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • provide the App's core features;
  • open, read, create, edit, and save note files;
  • maintain settings and app state on your device;
  • support app functionality, troubleshooting, and compatibility.

5. Data Sharing

Unless stated otherwise, the App does not sell, rent, transmit, or share your files with the developer or any third parties.

If a future version of the App introduces features that involve external services, cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, or other third-party processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Storage

Files and related data are primarily stored locally on your device, either:

  • in the App's default folder; or
  • in a folder you explicitly choose.

Any locally stored cache, settings, previews, or support files remain on your device unless you delete them, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • continue using the App and save files in the App's default folder;
  • choose your own folder for storing files;
  • revoke folder access through Android system settings;
  • stop using the App at any time;
  • delete your files, clear the App's data, or uninstall the App.

8. Children

The App is not specifically directed to children unless stated otherwise in the App's store listing.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version will become effective when made available within the App or through the App's store listing.
