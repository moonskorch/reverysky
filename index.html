Turn Dreams Into a Living Star Map

ReverySky blends dream journaling, light progression, collection, and map exploration into an experience built from your own dreams.

Shape Stars With Meaning

As stars level up, they gain Inhabitants, visible Landmarks from dream details, and tarot-inspired Boons that turn mood and memory into progression.

Follow Patterns Across Nights

Dream Stars connect through tags and time, helping clusters, echoes, and recurring themes emerge naturally across your map.

Player-Oriented by Design

Free to use, no registration, and works locally on your device. Notes stay yours as portable Markdown files, with offline access and no ads.
